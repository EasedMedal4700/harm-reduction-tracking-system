## â“ Common Questions

### "Can I recover my data after deleting it?"
**No.** Once deleted, it's gone forever. That's why we strongly recommend downloading your data first!

### "What if I delete my account by mistake?"
**You can't undo it.** You'll need to create a new account. If you downloaded your data first, you can manually re-enter it with the new account.

### "Will deleting my data delete my account?"
**No.** Deleting your data removes all your entries, but your account stays active. You can log in and start fresh.

### "Can I download my data multiple times?"
**Yes!** Download as often as you want. It's a good backup strategy.

### "What format is the downloaded data?"
**JSON format.** It's a text file that can be opened with any text editor. Developers can also import it into other apps.

### "How do I re-import my data?"
Currently, you need to manually re-enter it. A future update will add import functionality.
